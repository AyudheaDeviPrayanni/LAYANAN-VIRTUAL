About La Chill Bar


since 2013

One of the favorite holiday destinations in Lombok is Senggigi. Senggigi Beach has been familiar for a long time, including the cafes around it. La Chill Bar, one of the cafes that is very suitable to relieve stress. The cafe which is located on the coast provides a harmonious natural feel. The gentle sweep of the waves can calm the mind. Until the most awaited is twilight which has a very beautiful blend of colors. Accompanied by a menu that is suitable for Indonesian and foreign tongues. This cafe is used as an interesting instagramable photo spot so that it is in great demand by teenagers.
